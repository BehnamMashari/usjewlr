Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What's your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unworn, unaltered pieces in original packaging. Custom or engraved items are final sale.

Do you offer ring resizing?

Yes — complimentary ring resizing is included within 30 days of purchase. After that, we charge a standard resizing fee.

Can I create a custom piece?

Absolutely. Contact us with your vision and our design team will work with you to bring it to life. Custom orders take 4–6 weeks.

Are your diamonds certified?

Every diamond over 0.5ct comes with a GIA certificate. All stones are conflict-free and ethically sourced.

How do I care for my jewelry?

Avoid chemicals, lotions, and water. Store in the provided pouch or box. Clean gently with a soft cloth. We include a full care guide with every order.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 40 countries. International shipping rates and duties vary by destination and are calculated at checkout.
