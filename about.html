Our Story

Home / About
Established 2018

Crafted with Heart,
Worn with Pride

USJEWLR was born from a simple belief: that every woman deserves to wear jewelry as extraordinary as she is. Founded in New York City in 2018 by master jeweler Sophia Mashari, our atelier combines centuries-old goldsmithing techniques with contemporary design.

We source only the finest conflict-free diamonds and ethically mined gemstones, partnering with suppliers who share our commitment to sustainability and fair labor practices. Every piece that leaves our workshop has been touched by at least a dozen skilled hands.

From a custom engagement ring to a thoughtful birthday gift, we believe jewelry should tell your story — and we're honored to be part of it.
